📚 Health Education & Self-Care Guidance

🦟 Dengue Prevention

Keep surroundings clean and remove stagnant water to prevent mosquito breeding.

💧 Hydration Reminder

Drink plenty of water regularly to prevent dehydration during fever or illness.

🛌 Rest and Recovery

Get enough sleep and rest to help the body recover from infection and fatigue.

🌡 Symptom Monitoring

Monitor body temperature and worsening symptoms daily for early detection of complications.

⚠ Warning Signs

  • Persistent high fever
  • Severe headache
  • Frequent vomiting
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Extreme weakness
  • Worsening rashes

Seek professional healthcare immediately if these symptoms become severe.
